Formal Approaches to Slavic Linguistics 34

Welcome to the home page of FASL34, the 34th Formal Approaches to Slavic Linguistics conference! FASL34 is organized by the Department of Linguistics at Cornell University and will be hosted in Ithaca, NY, May 16th - 19th, 2025. All sessions will take place in person at Cornell University.

Important Dates

  • Abstract Submission deadline: January 20th, 2025 (Anywhere on Earth)

  • Notification of acceptance: Mid March

  • Conference date: May 16th - 19th
