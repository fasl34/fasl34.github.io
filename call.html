Call for Papers

Deadline: TBA

The Department of Linguistics at Cornell University is pleased to invite abstracts for submission to the 34th Formal Approaches to Slavic Linguistics (FASL34) to be held in Ithaca, NY, May 16th - 18th, 2025.

The FASL34 organizing committee welcomes abstract submissions for X>-minute oral presentations, followed by X minutes for discussion, and for poster presentations. Contributions to any aspect of Slavic linguistics, or comparison of these languages with other languages, are welcome.

Instructions for Abstract Submission

Abstracts are not to exceed two pages of A4 or letter-sized (8.5"x 11") paper, including data and references, with 1" margins on all sides and single spacing, set in a font no smaller than 11 points. The abstract should have a clear title but should not identify the author(s). The abstract must be submitted electronically in PDF format, using the electronic submission website accessible from: TBA.
*Submissions are limited to one individual and one joint abstract per author, or two joint abstracts per author.
